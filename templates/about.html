ABOUT

The professionals at NorCo Homebuyers have a varied and diverse skill set. While the company's primary focus is real estate, the main motivation is education and providing second chances. We recognize that real estate is one of the best vehicles for wealth creation, but to steer that vehicle correctly requires education and probably a couple of second chances. Education is a core value at NCHB because we feel that most, if not all, problems and fears can be eliminated with a little extra education. With a little extra education people can have a second chance, people can take control, and more importantly people can gain more freedom!
